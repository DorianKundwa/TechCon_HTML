Our History

Since its inception, TechCon has brought together technologists, entrepreneurs, and researchers to share breakthroughs and best practices. What started as a small community meetup has grown into a global gathering that spans multiple tracks and hands-on labs.

Our Mission

TechCon exists to accelerate learning, foster inclusive communities, and turn ideas into impact. We aim to provide practical content for builders and strategic insights for leaders, with accessibility and global participation at the core of our program.
